What is an Opt-In?

An Opt-in, also known as 'Conversion'

Is when a visitor to your website takes the desired action, such as inserting their email on your landing page.

What about optin rate?

The Opt-in rate is calculated by dividing the total unique amount of visitors who visited your link by the amount of people who took action.

How does it work?

Installing the tracking code ('pixel') presented on this page will allow us to track your opt-in rate.
